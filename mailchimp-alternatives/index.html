Best Mailchimp alternatives for 2026

Independent picks. Affiliate links noted.

Mailchimp is a strong general-purpose choice, but as your business grows or your marketing needs become more specific, its all-in-one approach can start to feel limiting. Maybe you need deeper automation, better e-commerce integration, or a simpler content-first approach. Here are five focused alternatives that excel where Mailchimp may fall short.

The best alternatives to Mailchimp

  • Kit
    Kit is built for creators who want email marketing to feel like a natural extension of their content, not a separate chore. Its clean subscriber management and automation tools are designed around newsletters and online courses, making it a favorite among bloggers and educators.
    Visit site: Kit →
  • ActiveCampaign
    ActiveCampaign stands out for teams that need more than just email sends: its powerful automation and CRM-connected workflows allow for highly personalized customer journeys. It is frequently cited as a top choice for B2B and B2C businesses focused on driving conversions through sophisticated segmentation.
    Visit site: ActiveCampaign →
  • Brevo
    Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) is a strong option for SMBs and European-market businesses that want email, SMS, and transactional messaging in one unified platform. It is known for competitive volume pricing and a straightforward approach to multi-channel campaigns.
    Visit site: Brevo →
  • GetResponse
    GetResponse appeals to businesses that run webinars or events alongside their email marketing, offering landing pages and email under a single subscription. It is commonly reported as a convenient all-in-one solution for companies that want to nurture leads through live and automated interactions.
    Visit site: GetResponse →
  • Klaviyo
    Klaviyo is the go-to for e-commerce brands, especially those on Shopify, that want to drive repeat revenue through behavior-based email and SMS automation. Its deep integration with customer data makes it a powerful engine for personalized marketing campaigns.
    Visit site: Klaviyo →
